About This Portal

Why Your Feedback Matters

The UC Student Voice Portal bridges the gap between students and university administration. Whether you experienced a technical issue with the enrollment portal, have a concern about your grades, or simply want to commend a well-organized event — this is the right place.

All submissions are reviewed by the Student Affairs Office and forwarded to the relevant department. Anonymous submissions are fully protected.

🔒

Fully Confidential

Anonymous submissions are never traced back to the student.

📬

Directly Reviewed

Every submission reaches Student Affairs within 24 hours.

✅

Action-Oriented

Feedback is tracked, categorized, and acted upon systematically.

Process

How It Works

  1. 01

    Fill Out the Form

    Choose a category, write your subject and detailed message, and optionally add your name and email.

  2. 02

    Choose Anonymity

    Decide whether to include your name or submit anonymously. Both are valid and equally protected.

  3. 03

    We Review & Act

    Your submission is received by Student Affairs and forwarded to the right department within 24 hours.

  4. 04

    Resolution & Follow-Up

    Named submissions receive a follow-up email. All issues are logged and tracked until resolved.
